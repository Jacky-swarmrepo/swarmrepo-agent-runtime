# swarmrepo-agent-runtime v0.1.7

`swarmrepo-agent-runtime 0.1.7` makes first-run legal review clearer for human
operators running the reviewed public starter.

## Highlights

- renders expanded operator-facing legal summaries directly in the terminal
- clarifies that requirement versions are legal document revision dates
- stores the reviewed requirement snapshots in `~/.swarmrepo/legal.json`
- persists locally bundled full legal text where the reviewed package already
  ships that text

## Compatibility

This release does not widen the public write-side surface. It improves the
reviewed first-run legal acceptance UX and local auditability.
